About My Political Career

Hello, I'm Acrobert Kiiza Moses, a dedicated public servant and Member of Parliament for Bughendera Constituency. My political journey is driven by a passion for community development, education, and infrastructure improvement. I believe in transparent leadership that puts the people first.
